The full process

Seven Days From Missed Calls To Booked Jobs

You do not need to learn software. You do not need to rebuild your company. We set up the system, show your team how to use it, and go live.

1

Onboarding Form

After payment, you fill out a short form with your service area, business hours, common job types, team members, pricing notes, and calendar preferences. We keep it simple and ask only what we need.

2

System Build

We build your missed call callback setup and your Housecall Pro account in the first three days. That includes services, schedule, dispatch rules, customer notifications, invoicing, and team access.

3

First Screen Share

We walk you through the system owner-to-owner. You see how calls are handled, how jobs are booked, and where everything shows up. You can ask plain questions and we make changes while you watch.

4

Team Training

Your team learns the basics: schedule, dispatch, customer notes, invoices, notifications, and what to do when a recovered job appears. No long training course. Just the parts they need.

5

Go Live

We test the whole flow, confirm call forwarding, and turn it on. From that moment on, every missed call is followed up fast and sent into your booking system.

What the two calls cover

Two Hours. Enough To Run The System.

Call One: Owner Walkthrough

We show you the full call path, job booking flow, calendar, dispatch view, invoices, customer messages, and daily summaries. You approve the setup before anything goes live.

Call Two: Team Basics

We train whoever answers phones, runs the schedule, or works in the field. Your team learns what to click, where to look, and how to keep jobs moving.
